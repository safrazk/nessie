Directions

If you're leaving from Tomnahurich Bridge in Inverness, it's a straight road along the A82. After 3.3 miles, you'll pass the entrance to Dochgarroch Lock (on your left). After about 10 miles, you'll see the Clansman Hotel on your right, which is directly opposite the Clansman Harbour. If you're travelling from Fort William direction, the Clansman is about four miles from Drumnadrochit, on the left hand side of the road.
